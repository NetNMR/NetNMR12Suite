Select the region to zoom by dragging a rectangle around the area of interest anyplace in the 2-D area. If try to select a rectangle extending outside of the 2-D area, the zoom rectangle will automatically truncate its borders to contain only the 2-D area. If the rectangle does not have any area in common with the 2-D area, it will be discarded. Once this is area is defined you can zoom to that area by right clicking and selecting this Zoom command.
