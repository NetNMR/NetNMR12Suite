View menu commands

The View menu offers the following commands:

Zoom Changes the display range to be where the zoom cursors are currently located.
Full Changes the display range to the maximum required to view all spectra.
Y-scale The Y-scale submenu has several items: Gobal to toggle on and off global y-scaling, Individual to adjust local Y-Expansion factors, Up, Down and X1 to adjust the Global Y Expansion Factor.
Data Classification The Data Classification check item (if supported at your site) will toggle the display of the document classification based on the most restrictive spectrum found in the current document. The data classification for individual spectra may be displayed by selecting the dataclass item in the Label drop-down selection in the Parameters Dialog.
Portrait Puts the page in portrait mode.
Landscape Puts the page in landscape mode.
Toolbar Shows or hides the toolbar.
Status Bar Shows or hides the status bar.
